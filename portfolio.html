Capstone AI Project

For our project, the problem we were faced with was transforming the conversation between startups and investors. Our solution to this is our Proposal Chatbot. The chatbot aims to streamline the interaction between the investment company and the startup company to help improve the efficiency of investing in startups. The basic explanation of how it works is that the startup begins by filling out a form with basic information and a file upload of their pitch deck. This pitch deck then uses OpenAI to analyze it and extract important information. Our tech stack included Node.JS, React, and OpenAI API.
